## English Description

**Short description (Play Store, 80 chars):**
Colorful Hebrew card game for the whole family - play vs smart AI!

**Full description:**

Tsivoni! (Colorful!) is a fast-paced Hebrew card game, perfect for families and kids.

🎴 How to play?
Match cards by color or number, use special cards to block your opponents, and be the first to empty your hand!

✨ Features:
• 2-4 players (you vs smart AI)
• Special cards: Skip, Reverse, +2, Wild, +4
• Smooth card animations
• Game statistics
• Works offline
• No ads, no purchases, no tracking

🎯 For all ages - simple, fun, and addictive!
